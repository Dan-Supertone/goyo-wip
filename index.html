HOW GOYO WORKS

Use GOYO to De-Noise, De-Reverb, and even De-Voice your audio.
We do this by separating audio into 3 channels for full control.

SUBWAY STATION

AMBIENCE

Ambience is everything other than the voice. A static hum, cars honking, a crowd chattering, music in the background, and everything in between.

VOICE DRY

Voice Dry is the voice in its clearest form. This is the closest you will get to a voice recorded at a sound-treated studio.

VOICE WET

Voice Wet is the echo, room sound, and other spatial artifacts associated with the voice. You may not need it always, but it’s there when you need it.
